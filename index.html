Portfolio

Magic Square

"Magic Square" is a captivating C project that explores the mathematical concept of magic squares within the realm of computer programming. A magic square is a square grid filled with distinct positive integers in a manner such that the sum of numbers in each row, column, and diagonal is the same. This sum is known as the "magic constant."
